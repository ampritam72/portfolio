About Me

Hey there, I'm Pritam, a 23-year-old Creative Graphic & UI/UX Designer from Bangladesh. I'm currently studying in the Department of CSE at Varendra University, while building my journey as a designer and developer. My creative path started with experimenting on design tools, and over time I began learning to code in HTML, CSS, C, C++, and Python. I'm still learning and growing in coding—not an expert yet—but I love combining design with development to bring ideas to life.

My main goal is simple: to design and build things that not only look visually engaging but also provide seamless user experiences. Whether it's crafting modern interfaces, developing functional web solutions, or experimenting with new creative concepts, I'm always excited to push boundaries and explore new possibilities.

Outside of designing and coding, I enjoy music, exploring creative content, and playing competitive games like PUBG Mobile. And yeah, I also love sharing my work and creative experiments online to inspire and connect with others.
